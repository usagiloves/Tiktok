## Ket luan nghiep vu da chot

### 1. Mapping `Trang thai/TH - HT` cho return/refund

#### `Chua ve kho`

- `RETURN_REQUESTED`
- `SELLER_REVIEWING`
- `AWAITING_BUYER_SHIP`
- `BUYER_SHIPPED`
- `RETURN_IN_TRANSIT`
- `SHIPPED_BACK`
- `PENDING`
- `PROCESSING`

#### `Can kiem tra`

- `WAREHOUSE_RECEIVED`
- `RECEIVED`
- `APPROVED`
- `REFUND_SUCCESS`
- `RETURN_OR_REFUND_REQUEST_COMPLETE`

#### `Tu choi`

- `REQUEST_REJECTED`
- `REJECTED`

#### `Da huy`

- `CANCELLED`

#### `Hoan Tat`

- API khong duoc tu dong set gia tri nay.
- Chi nhan vien kho duoc doi tay tren Lark sau khi kiem tra kien hang thuc te.

### 2. Rule `Ngay tao don`

- Don `ORDER`: luon lay tu `create_time`.
- Don `RETURN/REFUND`: uu tien `order_create_time`.
- Neu khong co `order_create_time` thi lay `create_time`.
- Neu van khong co thi moi fallback sang don goc trong DB.
- Khi da xac dinh duoc gia tri thi phai luon day field `Ngay tao don` len Lark.

## Nguyen nhan ky thuat da xac dinh

### A. Status map sai

File lien quan:

- `src/modules/sync/status-mapper.service.ts`

Van de:

- `REFUND_SUCCESS` va `RETURN_OR_REFUND_REQUEST_COMPLETE` dang bi map thanh `Chua lam hoan`.
- Status la hoac status moi dang fallback ve `Chua ve kho`, rat de gay sai nghiep vu.

Huong sua:

- Doi map sang rule da chot o tren.
- Fallback nen la `Can kiem tra` hoac `Chua phan loai`, khong nen roi ve `Chua ve kho`.

### B. `Ngay tao don` bi thieu

File lien quan:

- `src/modules/sync/normalizer.service.ts`
- `src/modules/sync/sync-engine.service.ts`

Van de:

- `normalizeReturn()` co tinh `orderCreatedAt` nhung khong luon set field `Ngay tao don` vao payload Lark.
- He thong hien nay chi va duoc field nay trong mot so case fallback tu don goc trong DB.

Huong sua:

- Tai `normalizeReturn()`, sau khi xac dinh duoc `orderCreatedAt`, phai set ngay `Ngay tao don` vao `larkFields`.
- Giu fallback DB nhu lop an toan cuoi cung, nhung khong phu thuoc vao no.
